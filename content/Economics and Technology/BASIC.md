Basic Consumer Goods Corporation, stylized as BASIC, is a convenience store chain in the [[Rosen Commonwealth]]. BASIC is the largest cooperative federation in the Commonwealth, with over 11,000 locations throughout the country. BASIC, unusually, is vertically integrated, and controls a significant portion of the artificial meat industry[^1]

[^1]: Interestingly, BASIC operates the Commonwealth's last slaughterhouse in New Derry.
