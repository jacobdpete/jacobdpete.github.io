Basic Consumer Goods Corporation, stylized as BASIC, is a convenience store chain in the [[Rosen Commonwealth]]. A "chartered" corporation, it operates with independent administration but significant subsidies from the federal government. BASIC, uniquely, is vertically integrated, and controls a significant portion of the artificial meat industry[^1]

[^1]: Interestingly, BASIC operates the Commonwealth's last slaughterhouse in New Derry.
