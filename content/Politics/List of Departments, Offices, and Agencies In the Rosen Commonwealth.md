>[!Summary]
>The executive branch of the [[Rosen Commonwealth]] is composed of dozens of different administrative bodies that manage the Commonwealth's needs. They are categorized in three levels: *departments*, in which most administration is devolved to [[republic|republics]] and is more accountable to the cabinet than the [[Secretariat-Directory]], *offices*, in which administrators are directly appointed by secretaries and is more accountable to the Directory than the cabinet, and *agencies*, which are more independent and operate with less political oversight.

# Departments
## Department of Foreign Relations
**DEPFOR**
**Secretary:** Adelaide Douglas
Responsible for administrating the Commonwealth's foreign policy, diplomatic corps, and operating embassies and consulates. 
## Department of Justice
**DEPJUS**
**Secretary:** Sozea Uyemura
Responsible for working alongside the Supreme Judiciary and Court of Appeals, administrating the courts, conducting investigation, and punishment.
## Department of Finance and Economics
**DEPFIN**
**Secretary:** Daniel Pelc
Responsible for administering the Rosen economy, issuing economic directives, and identifying financial crimes.
## Department of Health, Labor, and Welfare
**DEPHEL**
**Secretary:** Selam Malomo
Responsible for administering hospitals, care and medical research, labor relations and protection of trade unions, and welfare as well as wealth inequality.
## Department of Education, Culture, and Science
**DEPEDU**
**Secretary:** Joel Castro-Miranda
Responsible for administering higher education, sports and recreation, and scientific research.
## Department of Internal Affairs
**DEPINAFF**
**Secretary:** Joel Castro-Miranda
Responsible for administering relations between republics as well as relations between the federal government and the republics.
## Department of Agriculture, Energy, and Conservation
**DEPAG**
**Secretary:** Laurite Fischer
Responsible for administering land allocation, farming, national parks, and energy generation (notably the maintenance and transportation of nuclear weapons.)
## Department of Defense
**DEPDEF**
**Secretary:** Pamela Łukowska
Responsible for administering the Rosen armed forces, maintaining territorial integrity, and general national defense.
## Department of Transportation and Tourism
**DEPTRANS**
**Secretary:** Xian Yaling
Responsible for administering Rosen roads, rails, and airways, as well as the largely state-owned tourism industry.

# Offices
## Department of Foreign Relations Offices
### Office of Communications
**OC**
**Administrator:** Samuel Ventura-Nascimento
Responsible for administering and enabling communication within the federal government and in the public, operating cell towers and radio.
### Office of International Image and Perception
**OIIP**
**Administrator:** Helena Malinowska
Responsible for administering the image of the Rosen Commonwealth, effectively distributing propaganda and other information about the country.
### Office of Intergovernmental Organization Relations
**OIOR**
**Administrator:** Lara Evenson
Responsible for administering the Commonwealth's relations with major IGOs, like the [[International Union]], the World Bank, and the International Security Directory.

## Department of Justice Offices
### Office of Auditing
**OA**
**Administrator:** Arthur Clemens-Barreto
Responsible for administering the Commonwealth's finances and accounting services, ensuring funding is allocated responsibly.
### Office of Anti-Corruption and Ineffective Governance
**OACIG**
**Administrator:** Senichi Kataoka
Responsible for administering responsibility and accountability within the federal government, combating corruption and fraud.

## Department of Finance and Economics Offices
### Office of the Treasury
**OT**
**Administrator:** Anna Schottel
Responsible for administering the Commonwealth's treasury, including its gold reserves and certain aspects of managing the Ayan Credit.

**OFAL**
