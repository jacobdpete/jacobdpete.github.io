The International Security Directory (ISD) is a supranational intergovernmental organization responsible for conflict arbitration, coordinating peacekeeping and counterterrorism efforts, and composing and enforcing treaties involved with arms control. It was founded in 1996, six years after the [[Nuclear Crisis]], as an autonomous organ of the [[International Union]]. The ISD's primary goal is to prevent large scale conflict and the proliferation of nuclear weapons.
The ISD has three “permanent arbitrating members”: the State of Saya, the West Ayan Republic, and the Rosen Commonwealth.
