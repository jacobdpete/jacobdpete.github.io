The *Nova Sociedade* (lit. *New Society*) was a set of political, economic, and social ideals determined by Jorge Almeida to be the keystones of an [[Almeidaism|Almeidaist]] society. It was the first work by Almeida to outline core principles applied in Commonwealth governance today: collective leadership, collegiality, universal access to sustenance, housing, and education, and workplace democracy. It was written over a three-year period and published in 1983.
