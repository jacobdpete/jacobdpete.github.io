Nora is [[Avaya]]'s smaller natural satellite, fixed in orbit of Avaya at Avaya-Luna Lagrange Point 4 (L4). As a Trojan Moon, it is a kind of stellar object that is fairly uncommon and only seen in [[Iveka]]'s moon system. Nora has a diameter of about 560km, making it roughly one-sixth the size of [[Luna]]. Its exact history remains unknown, but the widely accepted theory is that it formed as a dwarf planet between Avaya and [[Dillaa]], and was captured by Avaya in the last 50-80 million years.
Nora is not permanently settled. The [[Rosen Commonwealth]] landed the first humans on Nora in 2046 under the Odyssey Novo mission. [[Saya]] and the [[West Ayan Republic]] have long-term plans to land humans on Nora, and the Rosen Commonwealth maintains it will establish a permanent colony on Nora no later than 2070.
