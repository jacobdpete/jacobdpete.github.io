The Rosen Restorationist Front, commonly known as The Reds, were a political faction and military force from 1858-1871. Claiming to be the sole legitimate representative of the [[Rosen Federal Union]], the Reds were a cult of personality centered on Joao Fidalgo, the final president of the RFU. The Reds had the least support domestically but had major support from Saya, which directly intervened in the [[First Rosen Civil War|Rosen Civil War]] on the side of the Reds until 1869.
