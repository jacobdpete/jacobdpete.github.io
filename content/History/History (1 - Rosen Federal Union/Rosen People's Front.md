The Rosen People's Front, informally called the Socialists, were a political faction and military force from 1857-1871, during the [[First Rosen Civil War|Rosen Civil War]]. Descending from social democrats, orthodox socialists, humanists, and communists in the [[Rosen Federal Assembly]], they had widespread support from the Rosen populace but suffered from chronic materiel shortages during the war, as they had no foreign support.
