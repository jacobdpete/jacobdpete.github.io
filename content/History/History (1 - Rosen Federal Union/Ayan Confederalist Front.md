The Ayan Confederalist Front, known as the Confederalists, were a political faction and military force from 1858-1871, during the [[First Rosen Civil War|Rosen Civil War]]. Descending from federalists, radical federalists, and anti-union democrats in the [[Rosen Federal Assembly]], they were the largest faction in the Civil War and cooperated with the [[Rosen People's Front]] (The Socialists) from 1866 to 1870 to gain an advantage over the Reds.
